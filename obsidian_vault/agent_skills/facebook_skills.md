## Facebook vs Other Platforms

### Why Facebook is Different
- **Community-Focused**: Conversation and relationships over broadcasting
- **Longer Content**: 300-500 characters optimal (vs Twitter's 280)
- **Visual-First**: Images get 2.3x more engagement than text-only
- **Demographic**: Broader age range (25-65), more B2C than B2B
- **Algorithm**: Prioritizes meaningful interactions over reach

### Content Strategy
- **80% Community**: Value, entertainment, conversation
- **15% Education**: How-tos, behind-the-scenes, insights
- **5% Promotion**: Direct sales, offers, announcements

## Posting Strategy

### Frequency
- **Ideal**: 4-7 posts per week
- **Minimum**: 3 posts per week (Gold tier requirement)
- **Maximum**: 2 posts per day
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Avoid**: Late nights (after 9 PM), very early (before 7 AM)

### Best Times to Post
- **Weekdays**: 9 AM - 3 PM (local timezone)
- **Peak**: 1 PM - 3 PM (highest engagement)
- **Weekend**: 12 PM - 1 PM only if content is lifestyle-related
- **Never**: After 9 PM or before 6 AM

### Content Mix (80/15/5 Rule)
- **80% Community/Value**: Education, inspiration, conversation
- **15% Behind-Scenes**: Team, process, culture
- **5% Sales/Promo**: Direct offers, announcements

## HITL Approval Thresholds

### Auto-Approve (Low Risk)
- Educational tips (generic best practices)
- Industry news sharing
- Motivational content
- Team celebrations (no client names)
- Engagement questions

### Requires Approval (Medium Risk)
- Posts mentioning clients by name
- Posts with specific metrics/numbers
- Posts announcing new services
- Posts with external links
- Posts with customer quotes
- Behind-the-scenes with client work visible

### Requires CEO Approval (High Risk)
- Posts mentioning revenue figures
- Posts announcing partnerships
- Crisis communications
- Competitive positioning
- Legal/compliance-related content
- Posts about controversial topics

## Content Guidelines

### DO ‚úÖ
- Start with attention-grabbing first line
- Use emojis sparingly (1-3 per post for visual interest)
- Include line breaks for readability
- Ask questions to drive comments
- Tag relevant people/pages (with permission)
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Add visual content when possible
- Respond to comments within 2 hours
- Use authentic, conversational language
- Tell stories, not just facts

### DON'T ‚ùå
- Post walls of text (use line breaks!)
- Over-promote (follow 80/15/5 rule)
- Use more than 7 hashtags (looks spammy)
- Post identical content to other platforms
- Ignore comments or questions
- Use all caps or excessive exclamation marks!!!!!!
- Post during late night/early morning
- Share without context
- Use industry jargon without explanation
- Post when angry or defensive

## Visual Content Strategy

### Image Guidelines
- **Size**: 1200 x 630 pixels (optimal)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: Under 1MB
- **Brand consistent**: Company colors, fonts
- **Mobile-first**: Readable on small screens
- **Alt text**: Always include for accessibility

### When to Include Images
- **Always**: Announcements, promotions, events
- **Often**: Success stories, behind-the-scenes
- **Sometimes**: Tips/advice (infographic format)
- **Optional**: Simple engagement questions

### Video Content (Gold tier bonus)
- **Length**: 30 seconds - 2 minutes ideal
- **Format**: Square (1:1) or vertical (4:5)
- **Captions**: Always (85% watch without sound)
- **First 3 seconds**: Hook viewers immediately

## Hashtag Strategy

### Primary Hashtags (Use 2-3 per post)
- #SmallBusiness (broad reach)
- #[YourIndustry] (industry-specific)
- #BusinessGrowth (aspirational)
- #Entrepreneur (community tag)

### Secondary Hashtags (Use 1-2 per post)
- #[YourCity]Business (local)
- #B2B or #B2C (business model)
- #CustomerSuccess (results-focused)
- #Innovation (if applicable)
- #DigitalTransformation (tech-related)

### Event/Campaign Hashtags
- Create unique hashtag for campaigns
- Keep under 20 characters
- Easy to spell and remember
- Check it's not already widely used

### Avoid
- Banned/restricted hashtags
- Irrelevant trending hashtags
- More than 7 hashtags total
- #Follow4Follow type spam tags

## Facebook-Specific Features

### Facebook Stories (24-hour content)
- **Use for**: Time-sensitive updates, polls, quick behind-the-scenes
- **Format**: Vertical (9:16)
- **Length**: 15 seconds max per story
- **Frequency**: 2-5 stories per day maximum

### Facebook Live (Gold tier advanced)
- **Use for**: Q&A sessions, product launches, events
- **Minimum length**: 10 minutes (algorithm favor)
- **Announce**: 24-48 hours in advance
- **Engage**: Respond to comments during live

### Facebook Groups (Community Building)
- **Create**: Industry-specific group
- **Moderate**: Daily engagement required
- **Value**: Exclusive tips, early access
- **Growth**: Organic invitation only

## Analytics to Track

### Engagement Metrics
- Reach: How many people saw post
- Engagement rate: (likes + comments + shares) / reach
- Click-through rate: Clicks / impressions
- Share rate: Shares / reach
- **Gold Target**: 5%+ engagement rate

### Growth Metrics
- Page likes growth
- Follower demographics
- Peak engagement times
- Best performing content types

### Business Metrics
- Website clicks from Facebook
- Inquiries originated from Facebook
- Deals influenced by Facebook
- Community sentiment (positive/negative comments ratio)

## Crisis Management

### If Post Gets Negative Attention
1. **Pause**: Don't react immediately
2. **Assess**: Valid criticism or trolling?
3. **Respond**: Acknowledge if valid, hide if troll
4. **Take Offline**: "Let's discuss in DM" for complex issues
5. **Flag CEO**: If brand risk escalates
6. **Document**: Log in audit trail

### When to Delete a Post
- Factually incorrect information
- Accidentally leaked confidential info
- Offensive content posted in error
- Client requests takedown
- Legal compliance issue

## Cross-Posting Strategy

### Facebook vs LinkedIn
- **Facebook**: More casual, community-focused, visual
- **LinkedIn**: More professional, industry-focused, text-heavy
- **Don't**: Copy-paste identical posts
- **Do**: Adapt tone and format for each platform

### Facebook vs Instagram
- **Facebook**: Longer captions, community discussion
- **Instagram**: Visual-first, shorter captions, stories
- **Sync**: Can cross-post but optimize caption for each

### Facebook vs Twitter
- **Facebook**: Longer form, conversational
- **Twitter**: Brief, news-focused, real-time
- **Conflict**: Different character limits and styles

## Compliance & Legal

### Always Required
- #Ad or #Sponsored if paid promotion
- Permission before tagging clients
- No false claims or unverified data
- Respect privacy and confidentiality
- Follow platform community standards
- Industry-specific regulations (finance, healthcare, etc.)

### Red Flags (Escalate to Legal)
- Medical/health claims
- Financial advice or guarantees
- Before/after photos (some industries)
- Testimonials without consent
- Competitive comparisons
- Regulatory/compliance mentions
