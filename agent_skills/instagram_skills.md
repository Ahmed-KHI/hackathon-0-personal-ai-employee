## #ï¸âƒ£ Hashtag Strategy

### Mix of 3 Sizes
1. **Large Hashtags** (500K+ posts): 3-5 tags
   - Example: #Marketing #Business #Entrepreneur
2. **Medium Hashtags** (50K-500K posts): 7-10 tags
   - Example: #DigitalMarketing #BusinessTips #SmallBusinessOwner
3. **Niche Hashtags** (<50K posts): 5-7 tags
   - Example: #[YourIndustry]Tips #[YourCity]Business

### Industry-Specific Tags
- **Core Tags** (use every post): #[YourIndustry] #[YourNiche] #[YourLocation]
- **Content Tags** (match post type): #Tutorial #BehindTheScenes #CaseStudy

### Hashtag Limit
- **Maximum**: 30 hashtags per post
- **Optimal**: 15-20 hashtags (better engagement than 30)
- **Placement**: Either end of caption or first comment

### Research & Rotation
- Research new hashtags weekly (Instagram search)
- Track which hashtags drive engagement (Instagram Insights)
- Rotate hashtag sets to avoid spam detection

   - `.edit_[instructions]` = revise per feedback

## ğŸš¨ Risk Management & Content Guidelines

### Content to AVOID
âŒ Never post:
- Confidential client information without consent
- Personal data or PII
- Political or religious controversial topics
- Copyrighted images/music without license
- Medical, legal, or financial advice (if not licensed)
- False claims or unverified statistics

### Copyright & Licensing
- Use only owned images or licensed stock photos
- Credit original creators when reposting
- Use royalty-free music for Stories/Reels
- Obtain written permission for client photos

### Brand Safety
- No profanity or offensive language
- Professional tone (even when casual)
- Fact-check all statistics and claims
- Avoid clickbait or misleading hooks

### Crisis Response
**If Negative Comment Goes Viral**:
1. Screenshot and save as evidence
2. Notify CEO immediately (HITL)
3. Do not delete unless clearly spam/offensive
4. Draft professional response for approval
5. Address publicly if warranted, privately if sensitive

**If Account Hacked/Compromised**:
1. Change password immediately
2. Revoke suspicious third-party app access
3. Enable two-factor authentication
4. Post Story explaining situation once secure

- Create visual graphic from text post
- Add authentic/behind-the-scenes angle
- Increase emoji usage and casual tone

### Task Queue Integration

- `task_type: "instagram_action"`
- `trigger: "visual_content" | "milestone" | "behind_the_scenes" | "weekly_schedule"`
- `content`: Relevant context (item name, description, milestone text)
- `instructions`: AI guidance from `_get_instructions()`
